    MAYFIELD HACKS

    In Person Hackathon / December 16th, 2023 / 10 Hour Event

    About

    Mayfield Hacks is a first-time hackathon organization composed of students from Mayfield Secondary School in Caledon, Ontario, Canada. Our very first hackathon will take place at the beginning of winter break and will feature students working together as groups or individually to create unique and interesting games that are relevant to our selected theme. The event will take place on December 16th from 10 am to 8 pm at [location: TBD]. It will feature numerous workshops where students can expand on their programming knowledge or learn more about the hardware components that they have the option of using to create their game. The number of participants per team will range from 1 (solo) to 4. The event will also provide lunch and dinner to all participants and will offer meals that suit everyone’s potential dietary restrictions.

    Register & FAQ

    Register Here

    Q: Do I need prior coding experience to participate in Mayfield Hacks?

    There is no experience required to participate in Mayfield Hacks! We are confident you will gain all the necessary knowledge you need during our workshops!

    Q: How are teams working?

    Teams will range from 1 to 4 participants. If you want to be a part of a team but don’t know anyone, don’t worry, we will combine you with others who are in the same situation!

    Q: What are the prizes for winning?

    We have amassed more than $100 in prizes which will range from gift cards to merchandise. Two teams will have the ability to win these prizes: 1st place and runner-up!

    Q: What’s the theme for the Hackathon?

    The theme will be released on the day of the Hackathon to keep everyone on their toes and to encourage creativity and spontaneous invention!
